Privacy Policy - Ambient Zenith

Last updated: April 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Ambient Zenith ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from viewers of our content. When you interact with our content on third-party platforms (TikTok, YouTube, etc.), those platforms may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

Our automated publishing tools access only our own account data through authorized platform APIs (such as TikTok's Content Posting API) for the purpose of uploading and managing our content. We do not access, collect, or store any data from other users' accounts.

3. How We Use Information

Any information we access through platform APIs is used solely to:

  • Publish and manage our own video content
  • Track upload status and publishing history
  • Improve our content and publishing workflow

4. Data Storage

We store minimal data related to our publishing activity (upload timestamps, video identifiers, publishing status) locally on our own systems. We do not store any third-party user data.

5. Third-Party Services

Our content is hosted on third-party platforms. Your interaction with our content on these platforms is subject to their privacy policies:

  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • YouTube: policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Some of our content channels produce content suitable for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
